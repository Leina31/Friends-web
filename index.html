Friends

"I'll be there for you"

"Friends" is a 10-season American sitcom created by Marta Kauffman and David Crane, which has met with great success in many countries.The story tells the lives of 6 young adults, Monica, Ross, Rachel, Chandler, Phoebe and Joey. The show is humorous, and you can hear laughter recorded in every episode.

How does it sart ?

The first episode begins with Rachel, who arrives at a café dressed in a wedding dress, where the five others discuss. She explains that she was supposed to get married that day, but that she didn't love her fiancé and had left him at the altar.

Monica was Rachel's best friend in high school, and Rachel tells her that she was the first person she thought of (although she wasn't invited to the wedding). So she moved in with Monica and started working. From that moment on, you can follow the adventures of the six friends for 10 seasons.s

characters 

Monica Geller

Courteney Cox

Crazy housekeeper and maniac, Monica's a cook.

Chandler Bing

Matthew Perry

An accountant locked in an office all day, Chandler likes sarcasm and dark humor. Is it because his mother was always flirting with dads on his birthdays or because his father is a singer in a transvestite bar ?

Pheobe Buffay

Lisa Kudrow

Leaving behind her a dubious childhood punctuated by the suicide of her mother,abandonment of her father and life on the streets, Pheobe is a masseuse with an uncommon pleasure of life.

Rachel Green

Jennifer Aniston

A former rich kid, Rachel made the decision to live independently by working as a waitress. She gave up a marriage she didn't want and moved in with Monica, and she is the mother of a little girl, Emma.

Ross Geller

David Schwimmer

Brother of Monica and secretly in love with Rachel since adolescence, Ross is a paleontologist, a profession that fascinates him but bores his friends. Divorced three times, he is also the father of two children, Ben and Emma.

Joey Tribbiani

Matt Leblanc

Failed actor, Joey gets rejected from audition to audition. He's Chandler's roommate, and they're both best friends. Although he's not very smart, he gets a role in the series "Days of our lives", which will be the pinnacle of his career.
